University of Michigan Scholarships

Marc Altschull Actuarial Award

Deadline: March 11, 2024

Requirements:

  • Have declared a major in Actuarial Mathematics
  • Have 90 or more cumulative credits towards an undergraduate degree
  • Have had at least one actuarial or related internship
  • Have an overall GPA of 3.7 or better
  • Have passed at least two actuarial exams given by either the Society of Actuaries or the Casualty Actuarial Society
  • Be a US citizen or Permanent Resident
  • Be enrolled as an out-of-state student
  • Not have previously received this award

Otto C. Richter Memorial Prize

Deadline: March 11, 2024

Requirements:

  • Be a graduating senior
  • Be currently majoring in Actuarial Mathematics
  • Have a GPA of 3.0 or above
  • Have passed actuarial exams

Thomas S. Solomon Actuarial Award

Deadline: March 11, 2024

Requirements:

  • Has declared a major in Actuarial Mathematics or a related field
  • Has an overall GPA of 3.5 or better
  • Has passed at least two actuarial exams given by either the Society of Actuaries or the Casualty Actuarial Society

Irving S. Wolfson Award

Deadline: March 11, 2024

Requirements:

  • Majoring in Actuarial Mathematics
  • GPA of 3.5 or above
  • Have at least one term remaining for you undergraduate degree
  • Have shown actuarial achievement through academia, employment, and/or extra-curricular activity

Natarajan Family Award

Deadline: March 11, 2024

Requirements:

  • Majoring in Actuarial Mathematics
  • GPA of 3.5 or above
  • Have shown promise of future leadership
  • Priority given to students on SAM Board

David G. Hartman Actuarial Scholarship

Deadline: May 5, 2024

Requirements:

  • Has at least one more academic year to complete an undergraduate degree
  • Has already declared Actuarial Mathematics as their major
  • Is either majoring or minoring in Business Administration
  • Is pursuing a career as a casualty actuary
  • Has a GPA of 3.0 or higher
  • Has passed at least one actuarial exam
  • Is a US Citizen or Permanent Resident
  • Submits two essays

Margaret S. Huntington Prize in Actuarial Outreach

Deadline: March 11, 2024

Requirements:

  • Have First-year or Sophomore standing
  • Be, at most, in your second term of residence
  • Have earned a grade of "A-" or better in one of the following courses: MATH 115, 116, 156, 175, or 185, OR
  • Have earned a grade of "B" or better in MATH 205, 215, 217, 285, 295, or 425
  • Be an undeclared Actuarial Math major

Dept of Mathematics Emergency Scholarship

Deadline: None, on a rolling basis

Requirements:

  • Current UM undergrad majoring in mathematics
  • Enrolled in the current term for 12+ credits
  • Have 4 or less terms left to complete your math degree
  • Have experienced an emergency situation which has impacted your financial stability
  • Demonstrate substantial financial need

Spencer Educational Foundation Undergrad Scholarship

Deadline: Wednesday, January 31, 2024

Requirements:

  • Seeking a career in Risk Management, Insurance, and/or Actuarial Science
  • Enrolled at an accredited college/university for at least one full academic year (two semesters)
  • Preferred: Majoring/minoring in Risk Management/Insurance or a related field of study (e.g., actuarial science)
  • Preferred: Relevant work/internship experience and leadership skills

CAS Trust Scholarship

Deadline: Wednesday, January 31, 2024

Requirements:

  • Must be currently attending a college or university as a full-time student and continue as a full-time student for the 2024-2025 academic year
  • Must have sat for at least one actuarial exam by March 31, 2024
  • Must demonstrate high scholastic achievement, strong interest in the casualty actuarial profession, mathematical aptitude, and communication skills
