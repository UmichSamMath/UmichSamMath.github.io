What is an actuary?

Actuary: A business professional who analyzes the consequences of risk.

Actuaries must pass a series of exams in order to become certified by either the Society of Actuaries (SOA) or the Casualty Actuary Society (CAS).

Why should I become an actuary?


  • Top Ranked Job -- #1 Job by Forbes
  • High Demand & Growth -- Growing industry with advancement opportunities
  • High Salary -- Top-paying job
  • Work-Life Balance -- Flexible hours, low stress, hybrid work
  • No Graduate School -- Work immediately after undergrad
  • Make a Difference -- Interesting and rewarding work
