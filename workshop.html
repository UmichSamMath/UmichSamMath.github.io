New Member Orientation

New to SAM? The New Member Orientation is an introduction to the actuarial profession and information about SAM. Included is an overview of the actuarial curriculum, actuarial scholarships, and advice for underclassmen/new actuaries.

Interview Workshop

Learn about what makes a successful performance in a professional interview! In the Interview Workshop you'll learn how to prepare beforehand, the types of questions to expect, how to structure your answers, and post-interview etiquette.

Second Round Interview / Case Study Workshop

Continuing the interview process can be an unfamiliar space. In the Second Round Interview / Case Study Workshop you will learn the common forms of interviews and how to approach each to best prepare for the next steps.

International Students Workshop

The International Students Workshop covers all things career-related for international students: work authorization (CPT/OPT), timelines, short-listing companies and approaching recruitment. It also highlights key advice to present your best self during recruitment.
