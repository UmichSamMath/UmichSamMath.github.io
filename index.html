Welcome
to
SAM!

Are you interested in business, computer science, consulting, data science, economics, mathematics, and/or statistics? Student Actuaries at Michigan is an organization for undergraduates and graduates interested in pursuing careers in the actuarial industry and other related fields.

We have built a collaborative, welcoming community of like-minded students focused on helping each other to leverage our strengths to further develop our skills and make an impact. Our resources and connections support the professional development of our members through recruiting events, networking workshops, exam support, mentorship, and more! We can't wait to see you at our next event!
