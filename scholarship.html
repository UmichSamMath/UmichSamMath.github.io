Dept of Mathematics Emergency Scholarship

Deadline: None, on a rolling basis

Requirements:

  • Current UM undergrad majoring in mathematics
  • Enrolled in the current term for 12+ credits
  • Have 4 or less terms left to complete your math degree
  • Have experienced an emergency situation which has impacted your financial stability
  • Demonstrate substantial financial need

Additional Scholarships

Spencer Educational Foundation Undergrad Scholarship

Deadline: Wednesday, January 31, 2024

Requirements:

  • Seeking a career in Risk Management, Insurance, and/or Actuarial Science
  • Enrolled at an accredited college/university for at least one full academic year (two semesters)
  • Preferred: Majoring/minoring in Risk Management/Insurance or a related field of study (e.g., actuarial science)
  • Preferred: Relevant work/internship experience and leadership skills

CAS Trust Scholarship

Deadline: Wednesday, January 31, 2024

Requirements:

  • Must be currently attending a college or university as a full-time student and continue as a full-time student for the 2024-2025 academic year
  • Must have sat for at least one actuarial exam by March 31, 2024
  • Must demonstrate high scholastic achievement, strong interest in the casualty actuarial profession, mathematical aptitude, and communication skills
